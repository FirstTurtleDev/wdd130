Riverrush Expeditions

At RiverRush Expeditions, our mission is to inspire adventure, foster connection with the great outdoors, and provide unforgettable whitewater rafting experiences that are safe, exhilarating, and accessible to all. We are committed to guiding every guest through nature’s wild beauty with professionalism, passion, and a deep respect for the rivers we ride.

History

Founded in 2012 by a group of lifelong adventurers and river guides, RiverRush Expeditions was born from a passion for the wild and a deep respect for nature’s untamed beauty. What started as a single raft and a dream on the banks of the Colorado River has grown into one of the region’s most trusted and thrilling whitewater rafting companies. With over a decade of experience, we’ve guided thousands of guests through roaring rapids and breathtaking canyons—always with safety, excitement, and unforgettable memories at the heart of every journey. Our mission is simple: deliver world-class adventures that connect people to the rush of the river and the power of the outdoors.
